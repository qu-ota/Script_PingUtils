Title: Ping Utilities
A couple small commands for administrators that enhances the detection of the pings of users.

Commands:

/getping <name>
ADMIN+ | Announces to the server the ping of the user specified. Replace <name> with the player's name.

/togglepingscore
ADMIN+ | Toggles a function that sets the player's score to their current ping. Useful on servers that need a different use of the Score portion of the player list.
